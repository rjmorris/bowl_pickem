Bowl Pick'em Contest, 2014-15

Participants pick the winners of 39 bowl games. They assign a confidence score to each game in the range 1-39, with every value used exactly once. Standings are based on the sum of the confidence scores for the correctly picked games.

In the graphic, the height of a bar represents the confidence score assigned to the game. The color represents whether the pick was correct. Hovering over a bar will highlight the corresponding game for all other participants. The highlight is larger and brighter when the other participant picked a different winner than the participant whose bar is being hovered over.
